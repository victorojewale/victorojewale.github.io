Hello, I'm Victor.

CS Ph.D. Candidate at Brown University

I work on algorithmic accountability, AI auditing, and evaluation infrastructure for large language models. My goal is to build rigorous, context-aware evaluation methods that make model behavior measurable, comparable, and auditable in real-world use.

Affiliated with the Center for Tech Responsibility (CNTR) and Data Science Institute (DSI), advised by Prof. Suresh Venkatasubramanian. I am also a member of the RISE Lab, working with Prof. Malik Boykin.

Previously, I was a Data Scientist at SeqHub Analytics and a research assistant with The Mozilla OAT Project. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from the University of Ibadan.
